What IFC Toolkit does

IFC Toolkit gives delivery teams practical ways to inspect model data, check information quality and prepare better structured IFC and COBie submissions.

Why it exists

Many BIM validation workflows are fragmented across authoring tools, spreadsheets, manual checks and specialist software. IFC Toolkit aims to make common checks easier, faster and more transparent.

Built for practical delivery

  • Reduce repetitive manual checks.
  • Improve visibility of model data.
  • Support consistent information delivery.
  • Help teams prepare better IFC and COBie submissions.
  • Make IFC data easier to understand and use.
